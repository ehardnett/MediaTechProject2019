<h2 class="body-title"><span>Act Two Returnship</span></h2>
<div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field__item"><p>Ready to make a comeback? You may have put your career on pause for your family. But your talent and drive didnât pause. They were just being applied elsewhere, where they were needed most. We appreciate the diverse perspective you can provide and the contributions you will make.</p>

<p><strong>Overview</strong></p>

<p>The Act Two career relaunch program at NBCUniversal is a 6-month paid, fixed-term role for experienced professionals returning to the workforce after taking time off for personal reasons such as caregiving. The program is open to women and men who have at least 5 years of professional experience and have been out of the paid workforce (in an office/corporate environment) for at least 2 years. Possible assignment areas include Data &amp; Analytics, Cyber Security, Software/application Development, Systems Engineering, Technical Infrastructure (virtualized platforms), Technical Project Management, Production Management, Operations, Facilities Project Management and more.</p>
